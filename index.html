Meta Portal
Portal TV
Some placeholder content for the collapse component. This panel is hidden by default but revealed when the user activates the relevant trigger.

“Hey Portal” is a wake word that lets you use your Meta Portal hands-free to make requests or ask questions using your voice. Unless you turn the Meta Portal device’s microphone off, the Meta Portal device listens for a wake word to activate voice services.

If voice services are available to you, say “Hey Portal” when you want Meta Portal to start a video call, check the weather or respond to other commands.

WhatsApp Accounts

If your Meta Portal is connected only to your WhatsApp account or your voice interaction occurs during a WhatsApp call, Meta Portal will still respond to the wake word, but your voice interactions are not stored or accessible. Voice interactions occurring during a WhatsApp call are not sent to Facebook servers.

“Hey Portal” availability varies. Learn more about “Hey Portal” availability.

Share

If you're managing photos using the Portal app:
  • You can upload up to 20 photos at once.
  • cannot add or delete photos when an upload is in progress.
  • Photos you upload from the camera roll will only be displayed in the Portal app and on your Meta Portal device.
To manage photos using the Portal app:
  • Open the Portal app on your mobile device.
  • Tap Photos.
  • Tap the album you would like to add or remove.
  • Tap Display to display or remove photos.
To create an album in using the Portal app:
  • Open the Portal app on your mobile device.
  • Tap Photos, then tap the photo plus icon in the top right corner.
  • Tap the photos you would like to add, then tap Add Photos.
  • Tap New Album.
  • Type your album name, then tap Create Album.
